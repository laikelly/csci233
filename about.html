Hello welcome to our Pokedex Project.

Pokedex is short for Pokemon Index. Here you will be able to access a database of stats, information, and pictures of various Pokemons. On the main page you will be able to search and view information on all the Pokemons. Click a Pokemon's card to see a more detailed version of it's information. On the types page you will be able to see Pokemons sorted by their types. To access your personal favorites and account information, click the account button.

This Pokedex project was made by Melinda Chen, Kelly Lai, Emily Wu, Darren Chen, and Ryan O'Gara for our CSCI-233 project.
