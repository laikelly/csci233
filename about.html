About Our Pokedex

Pokedex, short for Pokemon Index, is a handheld electronic encyclopedia device; one which is capable of recording and retaining information of the various Pokemon of the world. Using PokeAPI, we were able to develop our version of a Pokedex. With our Pokedex, you will be able to access a database of a Pokemon and their stats. On the main page, you will be able to search by name or type to narrow your selection. Hover over a Pokemon's card to see more stats. With our account button, you will be able to access your personal account information and favorites.

This Pokedex project was made by Melinda Chen, Kelly Lai, Emily Wu, Darren Chen, and Ryan O'Gara for our CSCI-233 project.
